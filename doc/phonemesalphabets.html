Phonemes and Alphabets

Phonetics and Alphabets are discussed in this page.

Please note that the phonetic symbols may look like same as English, they sound different. So if you don't know what phonetic symbols mean, then see the grapheme. Graphemes are the pronunciation of a phonetic symbol. We use graphemes everywhere in this documentation.

Alphabets(alfo)

Teko uses both Abugaidas and Segmental Writing systems. They are called Kaleri(kaleri) and Delemo(delemo) respectively. We use Delemo in computers and Kaleri in handwriting. If you don't know what these means, just use Delemo, it's the one English uses.
